About Us We're two guys from Kellogg trying to lauch a business by graduation
What is Housekeeps? Housekeeps is a company dedicated to making AirBnB guest stays more predictable by offering amenities to AirBnB hosts. We're also out here trying to make it easier for hosts to rent their homes and not have to constantly worry about replenising small items, like shampoo or soaps.
So how's it work? We ask you to fill out a short survey and based on your responses provide a subscription box of all the travel-sized items your guests will ever need.
